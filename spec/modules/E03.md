## Goal
Every 15s per campaign in ADAPT_* mode, compute new `dial_level` from 30-day rolling drop% and write to `t:{tid}:campaign:{cid}:dial_level`. Implements ADAPT_HARD_LIMIT, ADAPT_AVERAGE, ADAPT_TAPERED.

## Verification phase
1. With drop% < target, level rises by 0.05 every 15s up to max.
2. With drop% > target, hardlimit drops level by 0.2 immediately.
3. average mode oscillates near target.
4. tapered mode: at shift-start, target effectively 2x; tightens as shift progresses.
5. Floor at 1.0; ceiling at adaptive_max_level.
